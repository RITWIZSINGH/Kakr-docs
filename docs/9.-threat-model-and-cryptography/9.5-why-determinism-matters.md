# 9.5 Why Determinism Matters

Many security systems rely on:

* Risk scoring
* Behavior analysis
* Probabilistic decisions

These approaches:

* Fail under automation
* Produce false positives
* Are difficult to audit

PTERI systems answer only:

> “Is this cryptographic proof valid — yes or no?”

This makes the system:

* Predictable
* Auditable
* Machine-safe
* AI-compatible
