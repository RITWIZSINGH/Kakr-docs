# 10.6 Observability & Monitoring

PTERI infrastructure provides:

* Request-level metrics
* Latency and error tracking
* Blockchain sync status
* Rate-limit visibility

What is **not** logged:

* Private keys
* Seed phrases
* Biometric data
* Unsigned secrets

Logs are designed for:

* Operational insight
* Incident response
* Capacity planning

Not for surveillance.
