# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 29, 2026**

Welcome to the documentation website of Kakr Labs, Inc. We value your privacy and
are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how
we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use
our search functionality, or interact with our services.

## 1. Who We Are

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

- **Kakr Labs, Inc.** (A Wyoming Corporation)
- **Business Address:** 240 W. Old Country Rd., Suite 1, Hicksville, NY 11801
- **Email:** contact@kakr.ai

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, including any requests to
exercise your legal rights, please contact us at the email address above.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve your experience on our documentation site:

- **Analytical Data:** We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to track website traffic,
  user behavior, and engagement. This includes anonymized IP addresses, device
  types, browser types, and pages visited.
- **Search Queries:** When you use our on-site search functionality, we analyze
  search terms to understand what information our users are looking for and to
  improve our documentation.
- **Voluntary Information:** If you contact us directly via email, we will collect
  your email address and any information you choose to provide in your message.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

- To analyze website traffic and optimize user experience.
- To monitor and improve the relevance of our search functionality.
- To respond to your inquiries, support requests, or feedback.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (specifically via Google
Analytics 4) to collect and store information about your interactions with our
website. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when
a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our
website may not function properly.

To change or withdraw your consent for analytics cookies on this site at any time,
use the button below:

## 5. How Long We Keep Data

We retain the data collected via Google Analytics 4 for a period of 14 months from
your last visit, in accordance with our GA4 data retention settings. After this
period, the data is automatically deleted.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as the EU or certain US states), you may have the
following rights regarding your personal data:

- The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
- The right to request the correction or deletion of your personal data.
- The right to object to or restrict the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@kakr.ai.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your
data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However,
please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 8. Third-Party Links

Our documentation website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not
responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We
encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit.
